Biography

CHELSEA DINORATH ANTÓNIO DANDA, BORN ON FEBRUARY 24, 2000, IN LUANDA, ANGOLA, IS AN ACCOMPLISHED SINGER-SONGWRITER KNOWN FOR HER DYNAMIC FUSION OF R&B, SOUL, AND AFROBEAT. WITH AN ARTISTIC CAREER BEGINNING IN CHILD- HOOD, CHELSEA FIRST CAPTURED HEARTS WITH HER SOULFUL RENDITIONS IN SCHOOL ERFORMANCES, EVOLVING TO PROFESSIONALLY RECORD TRACKS BY THE AGE OF TWELVE. HER MUSIC JOURNEY IS ADORNED WITH MULTICULTURAL INFLUENCES AND PERSONAL NARRATIVES, MAKING HER A STANDOUT VOICE IN THE CONTEMPORARY MUSIC SCENE.
